# TikTok Ads — Examples

## Example 1: DTC E-commerce Campaign Launch

### Prompt

> Launch TikTok ads for our skincare brand. Budget $3K/month. Goal: website purchases. We have a product catalog and some UGC from customers.

### What the skill does

1. Recommends campaign structure: Prospecting (interest targeting) + Retargeting (pixel audiences)
2. Suggests Spark Ads with existing UGC (most authentic)
3. Sets up catalog for Dynamic Showcase Ads (retargeting)
4. Configures pixel events: ViewContent, AddToCart, CompletePayment
5. Provides creative brief: hook formulas, 3 ad concepts, and sound recommendations

---

## Example 2: Creative Strategy Refresh

### Prompt

> Our TikTok ads CTR dropped from 1.4% to 0.6% over the last 3 weeks. Help diagnose and fix.

### What the skill does

1. Diagnoses creative fatigue (same 2 creatives running for 21 days)
2. Recommends new hook variants on proven body/CTA structures
3. Suggests 5 new creative concepts using TikTok-native formats
4. Provides timeline: test new hooks for 3-5 days, pause underperformers, scale winners

---

## Example 3: Account Audit

### Prompt

> Audit our TikTok Ads account. Spending $5K/month with 1.2x ROAS. Need to get to 3x.

### What the skill does

1. Reviews account structure (finds 8 ad groups with overlapping audiences)
2. Checks pixel health (finds Events API not configured — losing iOS signals)
3. Identifies creative issues (all 4 ads are polished studio content — recommends UGC)
4. Finds budget spread too thin (no ad group hitting 50 weekly conversions for learning)
5. Produces prioritized fix list with estimated ROAS impact
