# Cold Email & Outreach — Examples

## Example 1: Design Outreach Sequence

### Prompt
> Design a 4-email cold outreach sequence for our CRO agency targeting VP Marketing at e-commerce companies doing $5M-50M revenue. Goal: book a 15-minute audit call.

### What the skill does
1. Defines ICP targeting criteria and personalization tier (templated with company-specific hooks)
2. Writes 4-email sequence: value-first intro (Day 0), case study follow-up (Day 3), social proof (Day 7), breakup (Day 14)
3. Generates 3 subject line variants per email
4. Includes compliance footer and unsubscribe link
5. Sets send timing: Tuesday/Wednesday 9am recipient timezone

---

## Example 2: Diagnose Low Performance

### Prompt
> Our cold outreach has 12% open rate and 0.5% reply rate. What is wrong?

### What the skill does
1. Diagnoses open rate issue: checks subject lines, sender reputation, send timing, list quality
2. Diagnoses reply rate: reviews email length (finds 280 words — too long), CTA (finds "schedule a demo" — too high friction), personalization (finds zero personalization)
3. Prescribes fixes: shorten to 75 words, add one personalized line, change CTA to low-friction question, verify email list, check SPF/DKIM/DMARC

## Example 3: Compliance Review

### Prompt
> Review our outreach campaign for GDPR and CAN-SPAM compliance before we launch in the US and UK.

### What the skill does
1. Checks CAN-SPAM: sender identity present, subject lines accurate, physical address in footer, unsubscribe mechanism working
2. Checks GDPR: legitimate interest documented, clear sender identity, opt-out mechanism, no personal email addresses, data retention policy
3. Flags 2 issues: missing physical address, no documented legitimate interest basis for UK prospects
4. Provides remediation steps for each issue
