# Social Media Strategy — Examples

## Example 1: B2B SaaS Social Strategy
### Prompt
> Build a 3-month social media strategy for our B2B SaaS analytics platform. We are active on LinkedIn and Twitter. Target: VP Marketing at mid-market companies.

### What the skill does
1. Defines content pillars: Educational (40% — analytics tutorials), Thought Leadership (25% — industry predictions), Social Proof (25% — customer results), Promo (10% — product updates)
2. Creates 3-month calendar with themes per month
3. Recommends LinkedIn focus (3-5x/week) with Twitter supporting (daily)
4. Designs engagement routine: comment on 15 ICP profiles daily before posting
5. Sets KPIs: 500 new followers/month, 4% engagement rate, 50 website clicks/week

## Example 2: DTC Content Calendar
### Prompt
> Create a weekly content calendar for our skincare brand across Instagram, TikTok, and email newsletter.

### What the skill does
1. Maps content to platforms: Instagram (carousels + Reels), TikTok (before/after, routine videos), Newsletter (weekly digest)
2. Creates weekly template with specific content types per day
3. Designs repurposing flow: blog recipe → IG carousel → TikTok tutorial → newsletter feature
4. Suggests UGC strategy: customer routine videos reshared as Spark Ads

## Example 3: Social Audit
### Prompt
> Audit our organic social performance across LinkedIn and Instagram. Follower growth has stalled and engagement is declining.

### What the skill does
1. Analyzes posting frequency, content mix, engagement trends over 90 days
2. Finds issues: 60% promotional content (vs 10% target), posting at wrong times, no engagement routine
3. Identifies top-performing posts and patterns (educational carousels get 5x engagement)
4. Recommends: shift to 40/25/25/10 pillar mix, implement comment-first strategy, post consistently at optimal times
