# Copywriting Frameworks — Examples

## Example 1: Landing Page Copy with Multiple Frameworks

### Prompt

> Write landing page copy for our email marketing SaaS tool. Target audience: DTC e-commerce brands doing $1M-10M in revenue. Key differentiator: AI-powered flow optimization.

### What the skill does

1. Selects frameworks: AIDA for page structure, PAS for hero section, FAB for feature blocks, 4Ps for social proof section
2. Writes hero using PAS: problem (leaving revenue on the table), agitate (competitors are automating), solution (AI-optimized flows)
3. Writes feature section using FAB for each of 3 key features
4. Writes social proof using 4Ps: promise (3x email revenue), picture (case study narrative), proof (specific metrics), push (free trial CTA)
5. Generates 5 headline variants using headline formulas
6. Writes 3 CTA variants with anxiety-reducing microcopy

## Example 2: Cold Email Sequence

### Prompt

> Write a 3-email cold outreach sequence for our CRO consulting services targeting VP Marketing at mid-market SaaS companies.

### What the skill does

1. Selects framework: BAB for email 1 (empathy), PAS for email 2 (urgency), QUEST for email 3 (education)
2. Email 1 (BAB): Before (conversion rate plateaued), After (client case study result), Bridge (our audit process)
3. Email 2 (PAS): Problem (competitor just redesigned), Agitate (every month without optimization = lost revenue), Solution (free 30-minute audit)
4. Email 3 (QUEST): Qualify (if your team has hit a conversion ceiling), Educate (share one specific insight), Transition (to booking a call)
5. Writes subject lines using headline formulas for each email

## Example 3: Ad Copy Variants

### Prompt

> Write Google Search ad copy for our wasted spend audit service. Primary keyword: google ads audit.

### What the skill does

1. Generates 4 variants using different frameworks:
   - PAS variant: "Wasting Ad Spend? | The Avg Account Wastes 26% | Free Audit in 48 Hours"
   - AIDA variant: "Your Google Ads Have a Leak | We Found $43K in Waste Last Month | Get Your Free Audit"
   - BAB variant: "From Wasted Budget to Profitable Ads | See What 200+ Brands Discovered | Free Analysis"
   - Direct benefit: "Find Your Wasted Ad Spend | Avg Client Saves $4,200/Month | Free 48-Hour Audit"
2. Follows Google Ads character limits (30 char headlines, 90 char descriptions)
3. Includes responsive search ad structure with 15 headlines and 4 descriptions
